# Style Guide

## Tone & Voice

- Write in third person.
- Maintain a concise, objective, and neutral tone.
- Use clear, direct language. Avoid jargon, metaphors, and subjective exaggeration.

## Structure & Format

Produce one paragraph, or two short paragraphs if the content requires it,
totaling 120–150 words.

- **Open** with a clear statement identifying the artist and their core practice.
- **Follow** with medium, process, recurring subjects, themes, and background details.
- **Close** with a specific, verifiable note about current or ongoing work.
- Front-load the most important information.
- If the draft exceeds 150 words, trim non-essential details first.

## Specificity Rules

- Name institutions, galleries, exhibitions, and awards in full.
- Anchor every milestone to a specific year. Never use "recently,"
  "in recent years," or "currently" unless the user provides and approves the detail.
- Include verifiable education and exhibition history only.
- Attribute superlatives: "first," "youngest," and "only" must cite a source.

## Prohibited Language

### Banned phrases

- "artistic journey"
- "work is distinguished by…"
- "exhibited in notable venues"
- "drawing comparisons to old masters and contemporary styles"
- "generation of artists" or similar generational framing
- Any vague claim about "challenging societal expectations" or an artist's "legacy"

### Banned practices

- Generalized praise without specifics
- Vague references to influence, impact, or importance
- Placeholder language: "a major institution," "a prestigious gallery," "a renowned collection"

## Preferred Language

| ❌ Instead of…                                        | ✅ Write…                                              |
| ----------------------------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------ |
| "recently"                                            | Specific year: "In 2023…"                              |
| "in recent years"                                     | "Between 2020 and 2024…"                               |
| "a major New York museum"                             | "The Museum of Modern Art (MoMA), New York"            |
| "a prestigious gallery"                               | Full gallery name + city                               |
| "one of the most important artists of her generation" | Attribute it: "named by _Artforum_ as…"                |
| "sold for a record price"                             | "sold for $X at [Auction House], [City], [Month Year]" |
| "a renowned collection"                               | Name the collection and institution                    |
